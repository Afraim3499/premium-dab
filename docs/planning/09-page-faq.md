## Product FAQs

### Is Premium Daab 100% natural?

Yes. Premium Daab is focused on natural young coconut refreshment without unnecessary additives.

### Does it contain added sugar?

No added sugar should be used for the core Premium Daab product.

### Is it a bottled coconut water product?

No. Premium Daab is positioned as a fresh young coconut experience, not a bottled coconut water brand.

### Is every daab the same size?

Natural products can vary. Premium Daab should be selected and presented carefully, but exact size may vary slightly.

## Delivery FAQs

### Which areas do you deliver to?

Premium Daab is launching area by area. Customers should check the Availability page to see whether their area is currently served.

### How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on area, stock, and order time. The website should show an estimated delivery window, and the team will manually confirm through WhatsApp.

### Is delivery charge fixed?

No. Delivery charge can vary by area and should be controlled from the admin dashboard.

### Can I schedule delivery?

For v1, scheduled delivery can be handled manually through WhatsApp after order submission.

## Ordering FAQs

### How do I place an order?

Select your area, choose quantity, enter your details, and click Place Order on WhatsApp. Our team will confirm manually.

### Is my order confirmed after clicking the WhatsApp button?

Not fully. Your order is created in the system and sent to WhatsApp, but final confirmation happens manually through WhatsApp.

### Why is WhatsApp used for order confirmation?

WhatsApp makes confirmation easier for Bangladeshi customers and allows the team to confirm delivery time, area, and availability directly.

### Can I order more than 50?

For large orders, use the Events & Bulk Orders page.

## Payment FAQs

### What payment methods are available?

Payment options can include cash on delivery, bKash, Nagad, or other methods confirmed manually.

### Do I need to pay online first?

For v1, do not require online payment unless the business activates it later.

## Events & Bulk FAQs

### Can I order for weddings or events?

Yes. Premium Daab can be positioned for weddings, offices, rooftops, gyms, cafés, and brand activations.

### Do you provide the Premium Daab cart for events?

The cart experience can be offered for selected events depending on availability, location, and quantity.

### How do I request a bulk order?

Go to Events & Bulk Orders and send your quantity, date, location, and requirements through WhatsApp.

## General FAQs

### Is Premium Daab available outside Dhaka?

Availability depends on launch area. The site should collect waitlist interest for future expansion.

### Can I become a partner?

Partnership inquiries can be handled through Contact or Events page.

### How can I contact Premium Daab?

Use WhatsApp, phone, email, or the Contact page.
